Tofu

Description

Hey Internet, this post is kind of a long time coming. SO MANY of my recipes either feature tofu or would pair perfectly with tofu. And when I say tofu in this instance, I mean crispy tofu.

Firm or super firm tofu that has a crisp exterior and a chewy yet soft (not too soft) texture on the inside. Itâ€™s amazing.

Ingredients

  • Super firm tofu
  • Soy sauce
  • cornstarch
  • brown sugar
  • hot sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Press Tofu
  2. Combine soy sauce and brown sugar
  3. Cube tofu
  4. Marinate in soy/ sugar mix
  5. coat with corn starch
  6. Air fry for 20 minutes
  7. Serve
