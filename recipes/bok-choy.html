Bok Choy

Looking for a fresh side dish to perk up weeknight dinners? Meet bok choy. Crunchy stems topped with tender leaves are seared, then steamed with garlic, ginger, and soy for a dish of light and flavorful greens thatâ€™s as satisfying to eat as it is quick to cook. Here's everything you need to know to make the best-ever bok choy:

Ingredients

  • Baby bok choy
  • Soy sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Quarter bok choy
  2. Add to pan on high heat
  3. Add soy sauce, water, and cover for 3 minutes
  4. remove from heat and serve
